Our Mission


ASU EPICS Shrimp Farming Team

At our core, we are driven to promote sustainable water practices along regions where water health is particularly vulnerable. For our project, we aim to provide an accessible water analysis mechanism to small-scale shrimp farmers located along the Mekong Delta in Vietnam.Our commitment is rooted in the belief that informed decision-making is the cornerstone of responsible resource management. By ensuring communities have easy access to accurate water quality assessments, we aim to elevate awareness regarding environmental impacts and foster a culture of sustainability. We aspire to be a catalyst for positive change, actively contributing to the preservation of public health and the resilience of water ecosystems.
